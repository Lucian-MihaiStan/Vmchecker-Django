VmChecker

VmChecker is a online forum where teachers from different subjects can publish homework for students.
It is an open-source project released by students from University Politehnica of Bucharest.
The purpose of this forum is to organize and facilitate the publishing of homework for students.
Do you want to collaborate?
VmChecker is a project started in 2020 when the CoronaVirus Pandemic started.
The forum evolved due to the hard-work of students that contributed.
Send us an email at: vmchecker@gmail.com
Based languages: Python and bash-scripting
